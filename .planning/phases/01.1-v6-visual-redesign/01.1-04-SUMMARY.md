# 01.1-04 — Review Loop Reskin (v6)

## What was built

Reskinned the four review phases to the v6 §R spec and wired the OLED night theme.

- **Task 1 (`useNightSurface.ts`, committed `400b137`):** pure hook returning `'oled'`
  only when `useColorScheme() === 'dark'` AND the hour is in the night window (≥19 or <6),
  else `'light'`. No timer, no global surface mutation — the review screen reads it locally.
- **Tasks 2 + 3 (`review/index.tsx`, committed in the v6 review reskin commit):**
  - **Intro:** × Exit, `StreakChip` (at-risk), serif `Display` "Vamos lá.", queue + est-min
    `Heading`, new/review chips (new = brand tint), audio-first helper line, full-width Begin.
  - **Front (audio-only):** gold 3px progress bar, "listen" mono, a 76×76 round gold
    (`colors.accent`) play button with the v6 shadow, a `react-native-svg` waveform
    (lifted-brand on OLED), italic serif "O que ouves?", the speed hint as a display-only
    faint mono line, and Don't know / Reveal ↓. No image, no word — audio only.
  - **Reveal & rate:** image, gender-railed headword card with gender chip, stress/usage,
    sentence card with ghost play button, "how did it go?" + `<RatingButtons onRate={handleRate} />`.
  - **Done:** filled-cobalt surface (`colors.brandFill`), white text, translucent `StreakChip`
    (continued — the 280ms fill is the celebration), "today's session" mono, "Acabou." Display,
    big gold serif `{reviewed}/{total}`, translucent stat block (Reviewed / Accuracy / Time),
    white Home button with brand text.

All existing review logic preserved unchanged: `useQuery(['cards','due'])`, `playAudio` +
front autoplay effect, `handleBegin/Reveal/DontKnow/Rate`, `reviewMutation` (`api.submitReview`),
`sessionStats`, accuracy/time math, and the `next >= cards.length → done` transition.
