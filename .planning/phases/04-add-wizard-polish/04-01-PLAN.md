    - On error: does NOT set pending_cards to discarded (fields are still valid; client can retry)

    - FieldsInput: z.object({ input_text: z.string().min(1), kind: z.enum(['word', 'sentence']) })

    Remove the old generateRoute.post('/') handler entirely. The old handler path (POST /) is replaced by the two new paths (/fields, /images).
