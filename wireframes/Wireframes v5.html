Greeting, subtitle, nav titles, stat labels, chart axis, word headwords — all black (or white on dark surfaces). Cobalt is reserved for emphasis: the big due number, the streak number in detail, key action labels, active toggles, tag chips.

Year gives enough density for the heatmap to feel populated even for ~6-month-old users.
Rating colors stay semantic Only place semantic color appears in detail. Cobalt for good is intentional — the good rating is the desired outcome, brand-coded.
Hero math LONGEST is all-time regardless of period scope (it's about you). RETENTION rescopes to the period (recent practice).

log · v4 → v5

What changed.

Mostly: less.

Streak +1 spring animation Number flip, 380ms, haptic. Replaced with the chip transition (280ms fill, no scale).
Milestone celebration overlays All 6 thresholds (7/14/30/60/100/365). Brief v3's overlays — gone in v5.
Gold hero band on home The streak card that read "13 day streak" with the 7-day dot grid. Replaced by 52px chip.
7-day dot grid Both on home and inside detail hero.
Retention curve / detailed stats Punted from detail view; deferred to V2+ Stats screen.
Streak corner chip 3 states, 1 transition (at-risk → continued).
Period toggle on detail Month / year / lifetime · default year. All sections rescope.
4-stop heatmap ramp Was 3 stops. #EFF3FB → #A5BFE8 → #5A8FD4 → #2E5BC8
Rating distribution chart Stacked horizontal bar. Semantic colors live only here.
Text rule Surface-driven. Cobalt restricted to numbers + key actions.

Open questions

  • At-risk threshold · brief says "after 6pm." Is this user-local or app-fixed? Léo on the train at 5:55pm gets a different chip color than at 6:01pm — fine for the founder, may need work for international users.
  • Chip width on multi-digit streaks · "12" sits at ~52px, "100" pushes to ~60px, "365" to ~66px. Confirm growth direction (rightward, or centered relative to opposite edge).
  • Settings · with overlays + animation removed, the "celebrations off" toggle is no longer needed. The motion-reduce row stays but the section may not warrant its own subscreen anymore.
  • Word-card gender bar · kept as the only flash of non-cobalt-non-black color on home (rose for fem, cobalt for masc, gold for common/sentence). Confirm this falls inside "text follows surface" — it's a 3px decorative accent, not text.
  • Share action on detail · still TBD what it produces. Hero card image is the obvious answer.

Removed since v4

  • Streak +1 spring animation · was 380ms scale-and-flip with haptic
  • Milestone overlays · all 6 thresholds with cobalt-tinted backdrop
  • Gold hero band · home streak card + 7-day dots
  • Settings escape hatch · celebrations + animation toggles (no longer needed)
